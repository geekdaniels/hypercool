Repairs and Servicing

At Hypercool, all repairs and servicing are performed by factory-authorised technicians from one of our 28 national service centres. In-home, drop-off and pick-up options are available dependent on your needs and product

Installation Service

We offer hassle-free installation for your air conditioner at a time to suit you. Our professional installation service is performed by factory-authorised technicians.

We understand that if you have a problem, and you need it corrected quickly to keep family life running smoothly. We’ll schedule a convenient repair time with you for one of our factory-authorised technicians to visit your home, assess the fault and perform a repair.
